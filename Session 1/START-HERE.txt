🎮 HOW TO USE - NEW FLOATING BAR!
================================================================================

METHOD 1: Keyboard Shortcut (FASTEST!)
  1. Open YouTube and play a video
  2. Press: Ctrl+Shift+Y (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Y (Mac)
  3. Beautiful floating bar appears at top of screen!
  4. Use the controls, then press Ctrl+Shift+Y again to hide

METHOD 2: Auto-Show
  1. Open any YouTube video
  2. Wait 2 seconds
  3. Bar appears automatically!
  4. Click the X button to close it

METHOD 3: Extension Icon
  1. Click the extension icon in toolbar
  2. Bar toggles on/off

FEATURES:
  🍎  Apple-style glassmorphism design
  💎  Transparent blur effect
  ⌨️  Keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+Y)
  🌐  Works on ANY webpage (not just YouTube!)
  ▶️  Play/Pause video
  ⏭️  Next video

  ⏩  Fast forward 10 seconds
  ⏪  Rewind 10 seconds
  🎵  See what's currently playing
  ⏱️  View playback progress
  ✨  Smooth slide animations

❓ NOT WORKING?
================================================================================

DO THIS FIRST (fixes 90% of issues):

  1. Go to: chrome://extensions/
  2. Find "YouTube Controller" 
  3. Click the reload button ⟳
  4. Go to your YouTube tab
  5. Press F5 to refresh the page
  6. Try the extension again

STILL NOT WORKING?
  → Read: DEBUG-GUIDE.md for detailed help
  → Make sure you're on a VIDEO page (URL contains /watch?v=)
  → Check browser console for errors (F12)
